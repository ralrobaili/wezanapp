# Wazzan 💙

A comprehensive iOS application designed to support individuals living with **Type 1 Diabetes** by helping them monitor blood glucose levels, track insulin doses, and reduce the risk of missed injections. By integrating with **Apple Health**, Wazzan automatically retrieves health data and provides timely notifications when blood glucose levels become too high or too low, empowering users to manage their condition with greater confidence.

---

## Overview

Managing Type 1 Diabetes requires constant attention and accurate tracking of insulin doses and blood glucose levels. Wazzan simplifies this process by offering a seamless experience that combines health monitoring, medication tracking, and smart notifications in one application.

The application integrates with **Apple Health**, allowing users to automatically sync their blood glucose data without manual input, making diabetes management easier and more reliable.

---

## Features

* 💉 Track insulin doses to prevent missed or duplicate injections.
* 🩸 Monitor blood glucose levels in one place.
* 🍎 Automatic synchronization with **Apple Health**.
* 🔔 Instant notifications for high and low blood glucose readings.
* 📊 Easy-to-read health history and monitoring.
* 📱 Clean and intuitive user experience designed for daily use.

## Purpose

Wazzan was created to improve the daily lives of people with **Type 1 Diabetes** by providing a simple, reliable, and user-friendly solution for monitoring blood glucose and tracking insulin doses. The goal is to help users build consistent treatment habits, reduce missed doses, and stay informed about critical changes in their glucose levels.
